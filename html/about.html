A parapharmacie is a specialized retail space that offers a wide
range of health, beauty, and wellness products that do not require
a medical prescription. It provides customers with dermocosmetic
products, vitamins, dietary supplements, baby care items,
personal hygiene, and natural remedies designed to improve daily
well-being.
Unlike a pharmacy, a parapharmacie does not dispense
prescription medicines, but it focuses on preventive care and self-
care, guided by professional advice to help customers maintain a
healthy lifestyle.
